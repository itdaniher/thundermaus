Materials: 
  Secondary
  Corona Shield
  2" solid core wire


Fully Strip 2" of solid core wire (black or red spool)

Wrap around corona shield so that one side points up

Place shield on secondary (may require extra hands)

Trim secondary to 4-8" of loose magnet wire

Strip magnet wire with lighter

Solder magnet wire to breakout point and corona shield

Rotate shield to pick up slack in magnet wire

CA shield in place on top of secondary
